Pitch Tiles Roofing Solutions

Our pitched tile roofs are designed to withstand Singapore's tropical climate while enhancing your property's aesthetic appeal. We offer various tile options including clay, concrete, and synthetic tiles.

Benefits of our pitch tiles roofing:

  • Excellent water drainage during heavy rains
  • Enhanced ventilation reducing heat buildup
  • Classic aesthetic appeal that increases property value
  • Durable materials with long lifespan
  • Customizable designs to match your architecture

Metal Roofing Systems

Our metal roofing solutions provide exceptional durability and weather resistance for both commercial and residential properties. We work with various metals including aluminum, steel, and copper.

Advantages of metal roofing:

  • Long lifespan with minimal maintenance
  • Excellent resistance to Singapore's heavy rains
  • Reflective surfaces that reduce heat absorption
  • Lightweight yet extremely durable
  • Environmentally friendly and recyclable

Composite Panel Roof Systems

Our composite panel roofs offer modern solutions for commercial and industrial buildings. These systems combine excellent insulation properties with durable weather protection.

Features of our composite panel roofs:

  • Superior thermal and acoustic insulation
  • Exceptional weather resistance
  • Quick installation with large panel sizes
  • Low maintenance requirements
  • Various color and finish options

Professional Roof Leak Repair

Our expert team specializes in identifying and repairing roof leaks in all types of roofing systems. We use advanced detection methods to find the true source of leaks.

Our repair process includes:

  • Thorough inspection to identify leak sources
  • Minimally invasive repair techniques
  • High-quality materials that match your existing roof
  • Comprehensive testing after repairs
  • Preventative recommendations to avoid future issues

For expert Roof Leak Repair Singapore services, trust our experienced team to provide lasting solutions.

Comprehensive Waterproofing Services

Our waterproofing solutions protect your property from water damage, moisture intrusion, and related issues. We offer both interior and exterior waterproofing for complete protection.

Our waterproofing services include:

  • Surface preparation and cleaning
  • Application of specialized waterproofing membranes
  • Sealing of cracks and joints
  • Drainage system installation
  • Quality assurance testing
